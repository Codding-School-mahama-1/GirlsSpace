Empowering Girls in Mahama Refugee Camps

Welcome to girlsspace, an inspiring online platform based in Mahama, Rwanda, dedicated to supporting and empowering girls in refugee camps. We focus on providing accessible resources, educational tools, and a safe community where girls can connect, learn, and thrive.

At girlsspace, we believe every girl deserves the opportunity to reach her full potential, regardless of her circumstances. Join us in making a difference and nurturing a brighter future for these resilient young women.

Our Key Initiatives

Empowerment Workshops

Interactive workshops designed to build confidence, leadership, and communication skills among young girls.

Digital Skills Training

Providing essential computer and internet skills to prepare girls for future education and career opportunities.

Mental Health Support

Access to counseling and emotional support resources to promote well-being and resilience.

Empower Yourself, Share Your Story

Join our vibrant community for girls in Mahama Refugee Camp. Explore courses, connect with peers, and share inspiring stories. Together, we uplift and empower.
